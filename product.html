About Nodo

Small armchair upholstered in stretch fabric, featuring a curved tubular metal frame fully padded with high-density expanded polyurethane and covered in fabric. The front and rear legs merge into the backrest, forming an interwoven structural element. The seat, supported by elastic webbing and padded with polyurethane, is also available in a Green version with metal spring suspension and plant-based materials.
